Case Study

Walgreens Balance R Rewards program makes a healthy partnership with Epsilon

Project

Targeting

Services

Data, analytics and modeling Strategic mapping

Industry

Nonprofit

The story

The Leukemia & Lymphoma Society (LLS) wanted to help its local chapters expand their School & Youth Programs’ fundraising efforts. They asked Epsilon to develop an analytics-based targeting strategy to help the chapters identify high-potential schools and improve their marketing effectiveness.

The Solution

Epsilon's Loyalty platform supports and routs data for each Balance Rewards member interaction, including online/in-store enrollments, online purchases and activity uploads from devices lnk FitBit. We also integrated Web services support real-time enrollment through multiple channels.

The Results

more than 150 million people have enrolled in the Balance Rewards program. The system processes 4M+ purchase transactions per day and 4B+ loyalty requests per year.
